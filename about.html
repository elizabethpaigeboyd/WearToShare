Planning an outfit for an event can be stressful and time-consuming, especially when you're unsure of the dress code or trying to coordinate with friends. Wear to Share makes outfit planning fun, effortless, and collaborative.

With our platform, you can
👗 Create Groups – Plan outfits with friends for specific events.
📊 Vote in Style Polls – Get quick feedback on outfit choices.
🎨 Build Mood Boards – Gather inspiration and define your event style.
🛍️ Shop the Look – Discover outfit recommendations tailored to your event.

Traditional group chats and social media don’t offer a structured way to collaborate on outfit planning, leaving many people feeling indecisive and uninspired. Wear to Share solves this problem by streamlining the decision-making process in a fun and engaging way. Whether you're preparing for a formal event, a casual get-together, or a themed party, Wear to Share helps you feel confident in your outfit choices—without the last-minute stress.

💡 Our Mission: To make event styling easy, enjoyable, and collaborative.
